Description of Formulas - ABI RGBs

The following describes each formula included in the ABI (Advanced Baseline Imager - GOES-16 through GOES-19) RGBs section of McIDAS-V. These formulas create displays of various RGB products that are useful to visualize different features. These are also derived formulas, meaning if a data source is loaded containing the necessary bands for a formula, they will be listed in the Field Selector under the ABI RGBs tree. For specifics about the usage of each of these RGBs (except ABI True Color RGB), see the CIRA VISIT Quick Guides.

  • ABI Airmass RGB (Band 8T, Band 10T, Band 12T, Band 13T) - Distinguishes between different air masses (e.g. warn, cold, moist, dry) and highlights potential vorticity anomalies.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 8T

    ABI band 8 (6.2um) temperature.

    Band 10T

    ABI band 10 (7.3um) temperature.

    Band 12T

    ABI band 12 (9.6um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.
  • ABI Ash RGB (Band 11T, Band13T, Band 14T, Band 15T) - Monitors volcanic ash and sulfur dioxide gas.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 11T

    ABI band 11 (8.4um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.

    Band 14T

    ABI band 14 (11.2um) temperature.

    Band 15T

    ABI band 15 (12.3um) temperature.
  • ABI Day Cloud Convection RGB (Band 2A, Band 13T) - Distinguishes between high and low clouds.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 2A

    ABI band 2 (0.64um) albedo.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.
  • ABI Day Cloud Phase Distinction RGB (Band 2A, Band 5A, Band 13T) - Monitors convection by evaluating the phase of cooling cloud tops.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 2A

    ABI band 2 (0.64um) albedo.

    Band 5A

    ABI band 5 (1.6um) albedo.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.
  • ABI Day Convection RGB (Band 2A, Band 5A, Band 7T, Band 8T, Band 10T, Band 13T) - Highlights strong updrafts and small ice particles in areas of convection to indicate regions of severe weather.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 2A

    ABI band 2 (0.64um) albedo.

    Band 5A

    ABI band 5 (1.6um) albedo.

    Band 7T

    ABI band 7 (3.9um) temperature.

    Band 8T

    ABI band 8 (6.2um) temperature.

    Band 10T

    ABI band 10 (7.3um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.
  • ABI Day Land Cloud Fire RGB (Band 2A, Band 3A, Band 5A) - Highlights fire pixels in red and differentiates between water and ice clouds.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 2A

    ABI band 2 (0.64um) albedo.

    Band 3A

    ABI band 3 (0.86um) albedo.

    Band 5A

    ABI band 5 (1.6um) albedo.
  • ABI Day Land Cloud RGB (Band 2A, Band 3A, Band 5A) - Distinguishes between water and ice clouds, making it easier to identify high and low clouds. This RGB also distinguishes between green vegetation and brown inactive vegetation or soil.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 2A

    ABI band 2 (0.64um) albedo.

    Band 3A

    ABI band 3 (0.86um) albedo.

    Band 5A

    ABI band 5 (1.6um) albedo
  • ABI Day Snow Fog RGB (Band 3A, Band 5A, Band 7T, Band 13T) - Distinguishes between snow on the ground and clouds.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 3A

    ABI band 3 (0.86um) albedo.

    Band 5A

    ABI band 5 (1.6um) albedo.

    Band 7T

    ABI band 7 (3.9um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.
  • ABI Differential Water Vapor RGB (Band 8T, Band 10T) - Analyzes the vertical distribution of water vapor in the atmosphere and highlights potential vorticity anomalies.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 8T

    ABI band 8 (6.2um) temperature.

    Band 10T

    ABI band 10 (7.3um) temperature
  • ABI Dust RGB (Band 11T, Band 13T, Band 14T, Band 15T) - Contrasts between dusts and clouds.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 11T

    ABI band 11 (8.4um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.

    Band 14T

    ABI band 14 (11.2um) temperature.

    Band 15T

    ABI band 15 (12.3um) temperature.
  • ABI Fire Temperature RGB (Band 5A, Band 6A, Band 7T) - Highlights intense fires by contrasting them from cooler fires.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 5A

    ABI band 5 (1.6um) albedo.

    Band 6A

    ABI band 6 (2.2um) albedo.

    Band 7T

    ABI band 7 (3.9um) temperature.
  • ABI Nighttime Microphysics RGB (Band 7T, Band 13T, Band 15T) - Distinguishes between low clouds and fog.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 7T

    ABI band 7 (3.9um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.

    Band 15T

    ABI band 15 (12.3um) temperature.
  • ABI Simple Water Vapor RGB (Band 8T, Band 10T, Band 13T) - Analyzes the vertical distribution of water vapor in the atmosphere.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 8T

    ABI band 8 (6.2um) temperature.

    Band 10T

    ABI band 10 (7.3um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.
  • ABI SO2 RGB (Band 8T, Band 10T, Band 12T, Band 13T) - Detects and analyzes sulfur dioxide emissions from power plants and volcanoes.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 8T

    ABI band 8 (6.2um) temperature.

    Band 10T

    ABI band 10 (7.3um) temperature.

    Band 12T

    ABI band 12 (9.6um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    ABI band 13 (10.3um) temperature.
  • ABI True Color RGB (Band 2, Band 3, Band 1) - Creates a true color RGB display by simulating a green band. This is based on McIDAS-X's ABITRUCOL function which uses the CIMSS Natural True Color method.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 2

    ABI band 2 (0.64um) brightness.

    Band 3

    ABI band 3 (0.86um) brightness.

    Band 1

    ABI band 1 (0.47um) brightness.
