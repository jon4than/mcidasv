Description of Formulas - AHI RGBs

The following describes each formula included in the AHI (Advanced Himawari Imager - Himawari-8 and -9) RGB Formulas section of McIDAS-V. These formulas create displays of various RGB products that are useful to visualize different features. These are also derived formulas, meaning if a data source is loaded containing the necessary bands for a formula, they will be listed in the Field Selector under the ABI RGBs tree.

  • AHI Airmass RGB (Band 8T, Band 10T, Band 12T, Band 13T) - Distinguishes between different air masses (e.g. warn, cold, moist, dry) and highlights potential vorticity anomalies.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 8T

    AHI band 8 (6.2um) temperature.

    Band 10T

    AHI band 10 (7.3um) temperature.

    Band 12T

    AHI band 12 (9.6um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    AHI band 13 (10.4um) temperature.
  • AHI Ash RGB (Band 11T, Band 13T, Band 15T) - Monitors volcanic ash and sulfur dioxide gas.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 11T

    AHI band 11 (8.6um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    AHI band 13 (10.4um) temperature.

    Band 15T

    AHI band 15 (12.4um) temperature.
  • AHI Day Convective Storm RGB (Band 3A, Band 5A, Band 7T, Band 8T, Band 10T, Band 13T) - Highlights strong updrafts and small ice particles in areas of convection to indicate regions of severe weather.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 3A

    AHI band 3 (0.64um) albedo.

    Band 5A

    AHI band 5 (1.6um) albedo.

    Band 7T

    AHI band 7 (3.9um) temperature.

    Band 8T

    AHI band 8 (6.2um) temperature.

    Band 10T

    AHI band 10 (7.3um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    AHI band 13 (10.4um) temperature.
  • AHI Dust RGB (Band 11T, Band 13T, Band 15T) - Contrasts between dusts and clouds.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 11T

    AHI band 11 (8.6um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    AHI band 13 (10.4um) temperature.

    Band 15T

    AHI band 15 (12.4um) temperature.
  • AHI Natural Color RGB (Band 3A, Band 4A, Band 5A) - Discriminates between water and ice clouds as well as vegetation versus brown inactive vegetation or soil.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 3A

    AHI band 3 (0.64um) albedo.

    Band 4A

    AHI band 4 (0.86um) albedo.

    Band 5A

    AHI band 5 (1.6um) albedo.
  • AHI Night Microphysics RGB (Band 7T, Band 13T, Band 15T) - Distinguishes between low clouds and fog.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 7T

    AHI band 7 (3.9um) temperature.

    Band 13T

    AHI band 13 (10.4um) temperature.

    Band 15T

    AHI band 15 (12.4um) temperature.
  • AHI True Color RGB (Band 1A, Band 2A, Band 3A) - Displays an image of approximately what would be seen from the satellite, with green vegetation, brown inactive vegetation/soil, and blue water.
    Input Parameter Definition

    Band 1A

    AHI band 1 (0.47um) albedo.

    Band 2A

    AHI band 2 (0.51um) albedo.

    Band 3A

    AHI band 3 (0.64um) albedo.
